3rd Brazilian Workshop on Smart Cities (WBCI2022)

The WBCI2022 happen between 31 July and 5 August 2022 in the city of Niterói/RJ (Brazil).

This work the WBCI happen as a Satelite Event of the 42º Congresso da Sociedade Brasileira de Computação (CSBC 2022).

WBCI is an international workshop organized in Brazil that aims to attract original contributions with an interdisciplinary approach, both in the field of technology and in the humanities, focusing on the development and use of computer systems and their relationship with the macro topic of Smart Cities (and its various variants). The event is an opportunity to bring together researchers, students and professionals who work directly or indirectly on this topic of Smart Cities, as well as the unfolding of the use of technologies in the urban context, also for smart regions and territories.

Topics of interest include, but are not restricted to:

  • Computational Intelligence and Citizens
  • Computational Intelligence for Logistics in Smart Cities
  • Computational Intelligence for Smart/Green Homes
  • Computational Intelligence for urban planning and engineering solutions
  • Cryptocities/Cryptocurrencies
  • Green computing, sustainability and energy efficiency.
  • Heuristic and exact solutions to problems in Smart Cities and decentralized environments
  • High Performance Computing for Smart Cities
  • Human Interactions and Smart Cities
  • Humanitarian Logistics and Reverse Logistics
  • Infrastructure, platforms and frameworks for smart cities
  • Modeling and data analysis for smart cities
  • Public and Cyber Security
  • Security and privacy of citizen data
  • Sensing and the Internet of Things for smart cities
  • Smart Cities and Internet of Value
  • Smart Cities in the context of Renewable Energies and Microgrids
  • Smart Cities with Smart Contracts and Blockchain
  • Smart Cities, Smart/Digital Cities
  • Software development techniques and methodologies for smart cities
  • Techniques and methods of real-time processing of data streams
  • Thematic applications (in public safety, health, education, mobility, urbanism, among others) for smart cities
  • Transport and Urban & Social Mobility
  • Urban Planning and Management
